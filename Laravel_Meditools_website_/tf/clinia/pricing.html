Patient Pricing Information

Clinia believes in the value of chiropractic care for you and your family toward the goal of achieving total health and wellness. Therefore, we have created a pricing structure that is flexible enough to allow you to get the Clinia care you need, no matter what your budget. We also accept auto accident (SGI) and workers compensation (WCB) cases that result in problems which can be addressed with Clinia care. For patients who have little or no Clinia insurance coverage, flexible payment programs can be arranged.
